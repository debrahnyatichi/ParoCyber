# Assignment 1: Pentesting Agreement

## Overview

This assignment required the creation of a **professional Penetration Testing Agreement** aligned with real-world cybersecurity consultancy standards.

The agreement ensures that penetration testing activities are conducted legally, ethically, and within clearly defined boundaries.

---

## Objectives

- Understand the legal importance of written authorization
- Define clear scope and rules of engagement
- Apply ethical standards to offensive security work
- Demonstrate professional documentation skills

---

## Deliverable

- **Pentesting_Agreement.md**  
  A complete penetration testing agreement covering authorization, scope, confidentiality, reporting, and liability.

---

## Key Learning Outcomes

- Penetration testing must always be authorized in writing
- Legal and ethical considerations are as critical as technical skills
- Clear documentation protects both the client and the tester
- Industry standards guide professional security assessments

---

## Disclaimer

This document is provided strictly for educational purposes.  
Penetration testing without explicit authorization is illegal and unethical.
